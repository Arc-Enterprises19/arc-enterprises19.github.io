Thirty-seven episodes in and no contestant has managed to answer the first question in David Striker's "WWTBA Millionaire vs the Clock".

After the first episode which viewers called 'underwhelming' and 'boring' the remaining episodes were moved from GBC One to GBC Four. More episodes were filmed and the show was moved again and now airs on GBC Eight.

Over 200 contestants have already sat in the seat, none of them getting past question one. Producers started looking for contestants in the audience and even from random people on the street.

He's the first ever contestant to advance from the first question.
